About me

Hi! I am a research assistant professor in the Department of Computer Science and Technology at Tsinghua University.
My research interests include natural language processing, deep learning, knowledge computing, and machine learning systems. Currently, I am focusing on exploring how to efficiently build effective large foundation models at the intersection between artificial intelligence and high-performance computing. If you are interested in joining us, please email me with your latest resume.

Before serving as an assistant professor, I was a Postdoctoral Researcher in the Department of Computer Science and Technology at Tsinghua University under the supervision of Professor Maosong Sun. I also got my bachelor degree and completed my Ph.D. program in the Department of Computer Science and Technology at Tsinghua University under the supervision of Professor Zhiyuan Liu.
